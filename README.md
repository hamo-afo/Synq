Synq is a mobile app that brings together the hottest trends from around the world in one place. Whether you're interested in viral YouTube videos, stock market updates, or the latest news, Synq has it all!

## 🚀 Getting Started - How to Use Synq

### **Step 1: Download & Install**

- Download Synq from your phone's app store (Android or iPhone)
- Or if using web version, visit the Synq website

### **Step 2: Sign Up**

- Open the app
- Click "Sign Up"
- Enter your email and create a password
- Enter your name and hit "Register"
- Done! You're ready to go

### **Step 3: Login**

- Open the app
- Login with your email and password
- You'll see the home screen with all trending content

### **Step 4: Browse Trends**

- **Home Feed**: Scroll through trending content from all categories
- **YouTube Trends**: Click the YouTube icon to see viral videos
- **Stock Trends**: Check the latest stock market movers
- **Political News**: Read breaking news stories

### **Step 5: Interact**

- **Like**: Tap the heart icon to like trends
- **Comment**: Click to add your thoughts
- **Share**: Share trends with friends
- **Open Link**: Click to read the full article or watch the video

### **Step 6: Search**

- Tap the search icon at the top
- Type what you're looking for
- Browse results instantly

### **Step 7: View Reports**

- Go to Menu → Reports
- See statistics and charts about trending topics
- Download reports as CSV files

## ❓ FAQ - Frequently Asked Questions

### **Q: Is Synq free to use?**

A: Yes! Synq is completely free. No hidden charges or premium versions.

### **Q: Do I need an account to use Synq?**

A: Yes, you need to sign up and log in to use all features.

### **Q: Can I use Synq offline?**

A: Limited offline support. Some cached content will be available, but to see new trends, you'll need internet connection.

### **Q: How often are trends updated?**

A: Trends are updated in real-time. New content appears as it becomes popular.

### **Q: Can I like and comment on trends?**

A: Yes! You can like any trend and leave comments to discuss with other users.

### **Q: How do I report inappropriate content?**

A: Tap the three-dot menu on any trend and select "Report". Admin will review it.

### **Q: Can I export my data?**

A: Yes! In Reports section, you can download trend data as CSV files.

### **Q: What devices can I use?**

A: Synq works on:

- Android phones (Android 5.0+)
- iPhones (iOS 11.0+)
- Web browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari)

### **Q: Is my data private?**

A: Yes! We only use your data to improve Synq. We never sell or share your information.

### **Q: How do I contact support?**

A: Email us at hamoafo@gmail.com or visit our GitHub page for issues.

## 🐛 Having Issues?

### **Common Problems & Solutions**

❌ **App crashes on startup**

- Try restarting your phone
- Uninstall and reinstall Synq
- Make sure you have internet connection

❌ **Can't log in**

- Check your email and password
- Use "Forgot Password?" to reset
- Clear app cache and try again

❌ **Trends not loading**

- Check your internet connection
- Try connecting to WiFi
- Wait a few seconds and refresh

❌ **Slow performance**

- Close other apps running in background
- Clear app cache
- Restart your phone

❌ **Can't find a specific trend**

- Use the search feature
- Try different keywords
- Check different categories
